Why Are Dogs Scared of Stroms?

If your pup gets scared of thunderstorms, know that they're not alone, as this fear impacts up to 30% of dogs. Where does dog storm anxiety come from? The answer isn’t clear-cut. One theory is that thunderstorms can trigger dogs with noise phobia, so loud thunder can cause anxiety — in fact, dogs can hear low-frequency rumbles before humans. Some vets think that dogs get uncomfortable shocks from static electricity buildup. Ultimately, different parts of a thunderstorm can cause fear in different dogs

Kennel Cough Symptoms

Symptoms can occur 2-10 days after exposure and include coughing, sneezing, and nasal discharge. Fatigue and fever can occur in dogs with more severe disease. The cough may sound like honking, and some dogs gag at the end. You should see your veterinarian if your dog isn’t eating, has a fever, has been coughing more than 10 days, is lethargic or coughing up white foamy material. This may indicate that your pup has a severe case that requires treatment, or may have developed pneumonia.
